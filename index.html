Who We Are

Have you experienced Larch Hill as a camper? Are you looking to give something back and have great fun at the same time. Then joining the Volunteer Campsite team known as the ‘Meitheal’, at Larch Hill might be just the challenge you are looking for.

Here at Larch Hill the ‘Meitheal’ manage the centre on weekends throughout the year. It is a great opportunity to learn new skills including instructing activities such as archery, rock climbing and high-ropes. You will have the opportunity to learn other skills such as construction, tree planting and maintenance.

The ‘Meitheal’ are there to assist our visitors to the Scout & Guide Centre and to contribute to the continued development of the centre. There is huge potential for personal development in many different areas as there is always something for everyone. It is a great opportunity to make new friends, have fun and work as part of a dynamic team.

If this appeals to you then submit an application. It is the volunteer staff that are at the core of the success of our leading Scout Centre, why not get involved.
